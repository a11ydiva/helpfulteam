WCAG Success Criteria Met by This Design

  • 1.4.10 Reflow: No two-dimensional scrolling needed at 320px width.
  • 1.4.11 Non-text Contrast: Focus indicator has at least 4.5:1 contrast and surrounds the entire control.
  • 2.1.1 Keyboard: Arrow keys, Home/End supported.
  • 2.4.3 Focus Order: Logical keyboard navigation.
  • 2.4.7 Focus Visible: Visible on all 4 sides of focused element.
  • 1.3.1 Info and Relationships: Tabs and panels properly linked.
  • 4.1.2 Name, Role, Value: Roles and states clearly announced.

Why Are Arrows Appearing in Some Browsers?

Native scrollbars or visual indicators may appear in Chrome, Edge, or Firefox depending on the platform or settings. These are not coded in this example, and we hide them with CSS while retaining scroll behavior.
